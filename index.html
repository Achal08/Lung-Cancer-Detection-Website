We have developed this portal as the outcome of our Final Year Project titled "Lung cancer detection and prediction using deep learning". We have presented our work by the means of this website. The project is divided into two parts - GUI part (Frontend) and the Deep Learning model (Backend). We have proposed a hybridized model comprising of ResNet and VGGNet. The accuracy obtained is 98.73%.

You can upload the CT images of your Lung/s to get the prediction whether there is cancer or not. We are coming with a beta version which will help you to get more insights based on the CT image you upload. Also there will be a feature to consult a radiologist for more accurate results.
